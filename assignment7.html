Plot

Mirzapur is an Indian crime thriller set in the lawless heart of Uttar Pradesh’s Purvanchal region. The narrative centers on the ruthless carpet-smuggler-turned-don Akhandanand “Kaleen Bhaiya” Tripathi (Pankaj Tripathi), his hot-headed son Munna Tripathi (Divyenndu Sharma), and the morally complex Pandit brothers, Guddu and Bablu (Ali Fazal, Vikrant Massey), whose fateful entry into the Tripathi world ignites a brutal chain reaction .

Mirzapur is a gritty crime drama set in Uttar Pradesh, centered around the power-hungry Tripathi family. Akhandanand Tripathi, aka Kaleen Bhaiya, runs a carpet empire as a front for his illegal gun business. His impulsive son, Munna, dreams of taking over. The Pandit brothers, Guddu and Bablu, get entangled in the Tripathi world after a wedding murder by Munna. As they rise in the crime world, tensions grow. Munna, jealous and violent, kills Bablu and Guddu's wife, Sweety, leading Guddu to seek revenge. In Season 2, Guddu teams up with Golu (Sweety’s sister) to destroy the Tripathis. After a brutal war, Guddu kills Munna and seizes control of Mirzapur. Kaleen survives but is left broken. Beena, Kaleen’s wife, betrays the family from within. Season 3 sees Guddu struggle to hold power, with Sharad, Tyagis, and returning Kaleen Bhaiya challenging him. Golu is kidnapped and tortured, while political games intensify. In the end, Kaleen regains strength, killing off rivals in a bloody purge. The war for Mirzapur remains unresolved. Mirzapur explores themes of power, revenge, betrayal, and family, with no clear heroes—just shifting alliances in a violent game of dominance.
